Aravind Ranganathan snips a play

Name of the play:The Last Straw. Author:Bosworth Crocker

LANE.
Say--where's the boss?
MRS. BAUER.
He'll be home soon, I--hope--Jim. What you want? [_He stands looking at her with growing sympathy._
LANE.
Nothin'. Got a rag 'round here? Dumb-waiter's all wet.... Lot of groceries for Sawyers.
MRS. BAUER.
[_Without lifting her eyes, mechanically hands him a mop which hangs beside the door._] Here.
LANE.
What's the matter?
MRS. BAUER.
[_Dully._] Huh?
LANE.
[_Significantly._] Oh, I know.
